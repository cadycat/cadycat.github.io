Description

We are an acapella band. We are two groups of 3 siblings (Cadence, Braden, Quinn; Teagan, Kiley, Brinley) who joind together to make The Neighborhood Besties! We are a band that wants to make amazing music just for you to jam out to and make you happy. We unfortunately aren't all allowed to show our faces
online so until then, our identities will remain a secret, though some of you may already know who we are. Our dream is to make it to America's Got Talent. We love making music for you, so we hope you love listening to our music. We may put our music on some streaming platforms, such as Apple Music, and Spotify, We will let you know on this website, and our YouTube Channel if that does happen. Our song that will soon be realeased,You Broke My Car, Is expected to come out on Youtube and this website on Semtember 15, 2020, and we will keep you updated on any delays or new songs. But until then, Enjoy!
